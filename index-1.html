A Few Words About Us

Few words about ongata line sacco.

Ongataline sacco is a modern well disciplines sacco that is in line with the governments Kenya Traffic Act that needs all Public Service vehicles to be in a sacco for proper control and management of matatu business. This sacco has 14 seaters and 33 seaters as part of the fleet that ongataline sacco manages. We ply the following routes Nairobi, Rongai, Kiserian, Langata, Magadi.
